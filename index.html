    Binalot sa Tondo translates to "Wrapped in Tondo." It reflects our commitment to serving Filipino meals wrapped in banana leaves, just like how they are prepared in Tondo, Manila.

    To place an order, simply browse our website and note down the items you wish to purchase. Our dedicated team will guide you through the ordering process, including providing information on availability, pricing, and payment methods.
